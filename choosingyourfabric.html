Choosing your Fabric

What to consider

For someone who has never done any sewing before, this can be a huge dilemm--after all, the average fabric store has hundreds of fabrics with prices ranging from one dollar per yard to upwards of twenty dollars per yard!



First, you need to know the function of the fabric. Is it going to be for a pouch? A fancy dress? A skin-tight leotard? Armor? Think about the attributes according to the function.



Secondly, how long does it need to last? Is it a one-hour Halloween party costume, or will you be LARPing in this all summer long? This is Durability.



And finally, what does it need to look like? Is it shiny? Dull? Textured? Smooth? Coarse? Warm?





What happens if you don't plan ahead? You end up with a massive fabric stash!
