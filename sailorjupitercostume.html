Sailor Jupiter Costume

This costume was a group cosplay effort for A-Kon in Dallas in 2008! All us girls had always wanted to be Sailor Scouts, so we each picked our favorite and got to work. For some of the group, this was their first ever home-made costume, so me and Tiffany (Sailor Moon) helped the others out a lot.
