Our Mission

We at Ember Costumes have a variety of experience in costumes, some self-taught and some formal, but we don't want you to have to go through the expensive trial and error that we did! Our goal is to provide new costumers with tutorials and resources to help them make detailed, cost-effective costumes and props. Whether you are an otaku, a LARPer, a movie-premier junkie, or just want to wear something different and fancy every now and then, we sincerely hope that this site helps you!



-Angela and John
