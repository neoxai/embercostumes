Pokemon Trainer Gold

This costume was a made for AKON 21 in Dallas, Texas.



The hoodie, hat, and backpack were all modified from store-bought garments, but John actually sewed those shorts from scratch--amazing!



Also, if you see those nifty pokeball pins on Angela's bag and on John's backpack, we got them from a booth at the con, but they sell them online here: www.sanshee.com. Those guys are really nice if you do see them at a con! I swear that once me and John got six and pinned them on us, everyone and their mom did the same thing. It was awesome.
