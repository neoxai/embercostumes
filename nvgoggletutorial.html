Sam Fisher's Night Vision Goggles Tutorial

(PAGE UNDER CONSTRUCTION)
This first tutorial will cover the basic LED goggles, and the basics of vacuumforming.

Overview





These are similar to the fittings I used as the lens pieces. Here's a link to the item at HomeDepot.com.





I love the LEDs at SuperBrightLEDs.com. They are a bit pricier than regular ones, but since I only needed a few, it wasn't a big deal. I recommend getting the widest angle LEDs possible. I used 5mm, 120 degree, Green.

The LEDs operated fine with 3 volts, so I ran them in series with 2 AAs. The batteries last at least a full weekend with intermittent use.

Vacuumforming

Here are some pictures of the vacuumforming process. Also note that the plastic pulled in this example isn't the final version. For the final one, the plaster and PVC fittings were pulled as one unit, instead of trying to attach the PVC afterwards. If you look carefully at the final version photos, you will see plastic webbing connecting the three lenses. This is normal for vacuumforming with plastic that is too thick into tiny notches, but I chose to leave it as reinforcement.
