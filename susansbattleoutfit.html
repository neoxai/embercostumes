Susan's Battle Outfit (Prince Caspian Movie)

Background Information



My brothers and I have always loved The Chronicles of Narnia, so naturally when the movies came out we had to make ourselves some awesome Narnian battle gear. I, as the oldest and the girl, get to be Susan of course. John, as the second oldest and a dude, gets to be Peter, and Nick, our youngest and rather surly brother, gets to be Edmund. We decided that the Prince Caspain costumes were totally epic, and so based most of our pieces on those outfits.

The best place to get reference images for this costume is Narniaweb.com: The Wardrobe Door. There is an entire page for Susan's Prince Caspian costumes and a page detailing her battle outfit, the one I picked to do.



This is an extremely labor intensive project, made up a bunch of very detailed pieces. John and I have been working on these costumes for over a year, and we're still not totally done! But hopefully you can enjoy looking at what we have done and learn a thing or two from it too!
